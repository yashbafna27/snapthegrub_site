It's easier to track what you eat. Count calories and nutrients by taking a picture. Snap the Grub will recognises the foods and log the nutrient. Also it will keep you updated with food facts and diet plans. Hence saving your time to maintain a better healthy lifestyle.

App Features

The app will help you to keep track of nutritional values of food by scanning the foods instantly and provide nutritional values. More over app will provide nutritional facts to keep you updated for better healthy life

Scan food

Just a click away. Scan the food, know the nutritional value.

Get updated with facts

Food facts will be delivered to encrich knowledge, hence a healthy life.

Know about diets

Get to know about different kinds of diet which may help you to adapt better one based on your body requirement.

Keep history of nutritional value

Your scanned food's nutritional value will be logged automatically by the app, so that later it will be handy to watch.

How to do food scan via app?

This is pretty easy to use.

  • Click on 'Scan Food' tab at below
  • Place the food over camera and check over preview screen
  • Click on 'Scan Food' button

That's simple, right?

How to check food facts?

Just be in home screen to check food facts.

  • Click on 'Home' tab at below
  • Food facts/Posts will be loaded to your screen

We will update you with facts. It's our word to you.😉

How to know about diet plan?

App contains details of the most frequently diet plans used

  • Click on 'Diet Plans' tab at below
  • Click on any of the diet from the grid
  • Details of the diet will popup

How to check food history?

All the scan food history will be automatically logged in.

  • Click on 'History' tab at below
  • If you have scanned any food before, you will get a list of food records
  • Click on any food from the list to open nutritional chart
